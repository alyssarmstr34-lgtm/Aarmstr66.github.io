Activities at Lake Powell

Watch a video about boating on Lake Powell

Lake Powell offers a wide range of activities for visitors to enjoy. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or family fun, there's something for everyone. Here are some of the top activities you can experience at Lake Powell:

  • Boating: Rent a boat or take a guided tour to explore the stunning canyons and hidden coves of Lake Powell.
  • Fishing: Cast your line and try your luck at catching bass, catfish, and other species that inhabit the lake.
  • Hiking: Discover scenic trails that lead to breathtaking viewpoints and secluded beaches around the lake.
  • Swimming: Take a refreshing dip in the crystal-clear waters of Lake Powell at designated swimming areas.
  • Canoeing/Kayaking: Paddle through narrow slot canyons and explore the serene beauty of the lake at your own pace.
  • Camping: Set up camp at one of the many campgrounds around Lake Powell and enjoy a night under the stars.
